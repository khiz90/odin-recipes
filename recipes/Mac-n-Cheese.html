Mac-n-Cheese

Description

This is a completely unpretentious, down-home macaroni and cheese recipe just like my grandma and mom always made.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 ¾ pounds whole-wheat macaroni
  • ¾ cup butter
  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • 6 cups milk, divided
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper salt and ground black pepper to taste

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  3. Melt butter in a large pot over medium-low heat.
  4. Reduce heat to low. Cook sauce, whisking frequently, until it begins to thicken, about 10 minutes.
